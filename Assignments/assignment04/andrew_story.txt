Tihana is a lecturer at Atlantic Technological University.

Atlantic Technological University has its headquarters in Galway and offers a wide range of courses.

Tihana teaches several computer science modules. Tihana is also the lecturer for the Web Services and Applications module.

In his lectures, Tihana explains how APIs work and how students can use Python to interact with services such as GitHub.

Tihana also demonstrates how automation can be used to modify files in repositories and commit those changes using scripts.

Many students learn practical programming and automation skills thanks to Tihana's teaching.
